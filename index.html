Facial login

Facial login allows users to quickly and securely log into their accounts using facial recognition technology. By simply taking a picture of their face, users can easily and quickly access their accounts without having to remember a complicated password.

Live tracking

Our live tracking system allows you to stay up-to-date on the status of your employee's activity its feature gives you real-time updates on the location of your field executives

Work History

The ESA app is implemented with a comprehensive employee work history management system to ensure accurate and up-to-date records of each employee's work history.

Attendance

Attendance Management System allows employees to clock in and out of the workplace with ease and accuracy. The system also provides managers with the ability to track and monitor employee attendance and absence records and can generate payroll details

Payroll

The payroll management system simplifies the process of managing employee salaries and wages. It allows employers to easily track and calculate employee paycheques, deductions, and taxes.

Task Management

Announcements
